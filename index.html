Wealth Management

Multi award-winning account aggregation software, moneyinfo is the client portal that delivers clients' secure access to their entire financial life in one place all beautifully presented under your brand. Workplace

Employers recognise the benefits of a workforce that is not worried about their finances. moneyinfo brings an employee's personal finances in context with their workplace savings delivering a unique app to help them make better financial choices regarding their spending, debt, savings, pensions, flex benefits and insurances.

Enterprise
